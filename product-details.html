About Magnesium Complex 5-in-1

Aiva Wellness Magnesium Complex 5-in-1 is a scientifically formulated supplement combining five synergistic nutrients — Magnesium, Vitamin D3, Zinc, Calcium, and Vitamin K2 — in a single, convenient tablet.

Magnesium is involved in over 300 enzymatic reactions in the body, supporting everything from ATP energy production to protein synthesis. Paired with Calcium and Vitamin D3 for superior bone mineralisation, and Zinc for immune defence, this complex ensures you get maximum benefit from every dose.

Whether you are an active athlete recovering from training, a professional managing stress, or simply someone looking to fill nutritional gaps in a modern diet, this formula is designed to fit seamlessly into your daily routine.

Why Choose This Formula?

Most single-ingredient magnesium supplements miss the cofactors that enable proper absorption. Our 5-in-1 formula is built around bioavailability — combining nutrients that work together, not against each other. Every batch is third-party tested and FSSAI-certified.

Take 2 tablets per day

The recommended dose is 2 tablets daily. Do not exceed this amount unless advised by a healthcare professional.

2
Take with or after meals

Take with food to enhance absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (D3, K2) and to minimise any chance of stomach discomfort. Evening is ideal, as magnesium supports relaxation and sleep quality.

3
Swallow with a full glass of water

Drink at least 200 ml of water when taking the tablets. Avoid taking with caffeinated drinks or alcohol as these can reduce mineral absorption.

4
Use consistently for best results

Benefits are cumulative. Use for a minimum of 8–12 weeks for noticeable improvements in energy, sleep, and muscle comfort. Store below 30°C in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
